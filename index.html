About Dr. Chandrashekar

Leading Interventional Cardiologist in Delhi

Dr. Chandrashekar is a highly experienced Interventional Cardiologist with over 26+ years of experience in cardiology. He has performed more than 26,000 cardiac procedures with exceptional expertise and care.

He currently serves as Associate Director at Max Super Speciality Hospital, Shalimar Bagh, New Delhi, and is also available at his private clinic — North West Heart Centre in Ashok Vihar.
